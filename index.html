Ministry of Education of The Republic of Trinidad and Tobago

Application for admission into a public primary school.

Criteria for acceptance into Infant Year 1 (as stated in Circular Memorandum No.2 of 2019):

  1. Child must have attained the age of five in the term in which he/she is to be admitted.
  2. Child resides within three kilometres of the school to which application is made.

Child's Information

The information required in this section must be for the child applying to be admitted to public primary school for the first time.

Family Information (Parents/Guardians)

The information entered in this section pertains to the immediate legal guardians of the applying child.

Siblings

The information entered in this section pertains to the siblings of the applying child.

Kindly note that having siblings at the school does not guarantee acceptance.

Document Upload

In this section you will be required to upload a recent utility bill, the child's birth certificate, and the child's updated immunization card.

Declaration by Applicant

By checking the box below you acknowledge the information submitted on this page is true, and correct.

I acknowledge that:

  • A declaration of any false information;
  • Failure to provide the documents requested, or
  • Failure to satisfy the stated criteria for acceptance

may disqualify my child from admission to this school.

Please note that incomplete applications will not be processed. Additional information will be required from successful applicants at registration. Parents are advised to apply to at least three (3) schools as an application does not guaranteee acceptance. All complete applications must be submitted to the respective schools by 20th March 2020.

Parents/Guardians of successful applicants will be informed during the period 15th May - 29th May 2020.
